Quadratic Equations Practice Game

How to Play

This game will help you practice solving quadratic equations through a three-step process.

Choose how many problems you want to attempt (5, 10, 20, or MAX for all available problems).

For each problem, you will go through three steps:

  1. Rewrite the equation: The equation is given with terms on both sides. Move all terms to one side so it takes the form \(Ax^2 + Bx + C = 0\).
  2. Factorize the equation: Factor the resulting quadratic into \((\dots)(\dots) = 0\).
  3. Solve for x: From the factorized equation, find the solutions \(x = \dots\).

Answer correctly to earn points. Incorrect answers will deduct points. Try to achieve the highest possible score!
